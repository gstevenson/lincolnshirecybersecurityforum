Who We Are

The Forum is part of a national network of forums and clusters set up under the UK Cyber Security Forum umbrella.

The formation of Lincolnshire Cyber Security Forum (‘LCSF’) is a collaboration between Lincolnshire Police, the Police East Midlands Special Operation Unit, the Federation of Small Businesses, LAGAT and other local businesses.

The objectives of the LCSF are to raise awareness of cyber security threats and issues, share and promote best practice, and highlight related opportunities within the business community in Lincolnshire, and within the cyber security professionals supporting those businesses.

To pursue its objectives, LCSF will seek to enable an ongoing programme of events to provide information about national and local developments and practices to both the general business audience and the cyber security professional audience, and enable them to interact with cyber students, trainees and others engaged in digital activities.
